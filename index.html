QR Code Generator — Features

Ek powerful QR code generator jo completely offline kaam karta hai. Kisi bhi type ka QR banao, color customize karo, aur PNG mein download karo.

  • URL / Website Link — koi bhi web link ka QR banao
  • Plain Text — koi bhi text QR mein convert karo
  • Email — direct email QR with subject & body
  • Phone Number — ek scan mein call ready
  • SMS — pre-filled message ke saath
  • Corner Markers — easy scanning ke liye styled markers
  • Color Picker — foreground & background custom karo
  • PNG Download — high quality image export
